Description

Assassinate your targets with clean headshots in this award-winning sniper game!

Tyler is an ordinary guy whose patience gets pushed too far. After getting fired as a supermarket cleaner he begins a violent career as a professional hitman. This, however, is also the beginning of social decline, false friends and a spiral of deception. Watch out! You cant trust anyone

Kotaku:
"Clear Vision is way better than it has any rights to be. I love that about the game."

Features:
Captivating Storyline
Animated Cutscenes
Blood and gore animation
Daily newspaper
25 missions
5 sniper rifles
Wind and distance calculation
Underground Boxing
Based on the "Clear Vision" flash game

Assassinate your targets with clean headshots in this award-winning sniper game!
